What Is Cowork?

Cowork is an AI-powered assistant built into the Claude desktop app. It works like having a capable teammate who can read your files, create documents, analyze data, browse the web, and connect to the tools you already use — all through a simple conversation. No coding required.

💬

Talk, Don't Code

Describe what you need in plain English. Cowork understands context, asks clarifying questions, and delivers polished outputs.

📁

Works With Your Files

Point it at a folder on your computer. It can read, edit, and create Word docs, spreadsheets, presentations, PDFs, and more.

🔌

Connects to Your Tools

Plug in Jira, Confluence, Figma, BigQuery, Slack, and other tools through simple connectors — no API keys or setup scripts needed.

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Cowork

Be specific about what you want
Instead of "make a deck," try "Create a 10-slide presentation for our leadership team showing Q4 corridor performance, with charts comparing volume and revenue by region." The more context you give, the better the output.
Iterate like you would with a colleague
Cowork remembers your full conversation. After the first draft, just say "Make the executive summary shorter" or "Add a slide about mobile engagement" — no need to start over.
Point it at your files
Select your project folder when starting a session. Cowork can read your existing documents, spreadsheets, and data files — so it works with your real context, not generic templates.
Let it ask you questions
Cowork will often ask clarifying questions before diving in. This isn't slowing you down — it's making sure the output is right the first time. Good questions lead to better documents.
Use it for the tedious parts
Cowork is best at taking your strategic thinking and turning it into polished artifacts. You bring the ideas and decisions; Cowork handles the formatting, structuring, and production.

Common Questions

Do I need any technical skills to use Cowork?
No. Cowork is designed for non-technical users. You interact through natural conversation — just describe what you need in plain English. If Cowork needs more detail, it will ask follow-up questions.
Is my data secure?
Cowork runs in a secure sandbox on your local machine. Files stay on your computer. Connectors use standard OAuth with your company's SSO. Your admin controls which tools are available and can set usage policies through the Teams admin panel.
Do I need a separate license for Cowork?
No. Cowork is included in every Claude Teams seat at no additional cost. Standard seats ($25/mo) include full Cowork access — the only difference between Standard and Premium ($150/mo) is usage volume. Start with Standard and upgrade only if you hit rate limits regularly.
Can Cowork replace my existing tools?
Cowork doesn't replace Jira, Figma, or Confluence — it connects to them and makes you more productive with them. Think of it as an intelligent layer on top of your existing toolset that handles the production work.
What happens when I hit my usage limit?
Usage limits are per-person and reset on a rolling 7-day cycle. If your admin has enabled overage billing, you can keep working at API rates. Otherwise, you wait for the reset. For most PMs and marketers, the Standard seat limits are more than sufficient for daily use.
